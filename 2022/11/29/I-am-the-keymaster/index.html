I am the Keymaster

Are you the Gatekeeper?

There are times when being a digital packrat comes in handy, this was one. I’ve had several old GPG keys floating around that I no longer used. Well, let’s be clear I never really use GPG, I just keep working with it since I find it interesting and it may actually come in handy one day.

I was able to find the old keyring and send out revocations for all of the old ones and update the keyservers that are still active with my current one.

And while I was at it, I did a bit of clean-up on keybase though it’s essentially a zombie site at this point. While searching for alternatives I came across keyoxide which seems to be the best way to link different accounts under a verifiable identity at this point. That’s all configured for now and I setup Web Key Directory entries on this site.
